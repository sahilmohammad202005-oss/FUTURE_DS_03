# Marketing Funnel & Lead Conversion Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project was developed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship at Future Interns.

The objective of this project is to analyze lead generation and conversion performance using Power BI. The dashboard helps identify conversion trends, high-performing lead sources, and areas where potential customers drop off in the marketing funnel.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze lead conversion performance
- Measure overall conversion rates
- Identify top-performing lead sources
- Understand customer acquisition patterns
- Detect drop-off points in the marketing funnel
- Generate actionable business insights

---

## Dataset

**Dataset Used:** Lead Scoring Dataset

The dataset contains information about:
- Lead Sources
- Lead Origins
- Website Visits
- Customer Occupations
- Geographic Locations
- Lead Conversion Status

Total Records Analyzed: **9,240 Leads**

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks:

- Total Leads
- Converted Leads
- Non-Converted Leads
- Conversion Rate

---

## Dashboard Visualizations

### KPI Cards
Displays:
- Total Leads
- Converted Leads
- Non-Converted Leads
- Conversion Rate

### Funnel Analysis
Shows the progression from:

Total Leads → Converted Leads

This helps visualize conversion efficiency and drop-off rates.

### Lead Source Analysis
Analyzes conversions generated from different lead sources such as:
- Google
- Direct Traffic
- Organic Search
- Referral Sources
- Social Media

### Conversion Distribution
Displays the proportion of:
- Converted Leads
- Non-Converted Leads

### Interactive Filters
Users can filter results by:
- Lead Source
- Lead Origin
- City
- Occupation

---

## Key Insights

### Conversion Performance
- Total Leads: ~9K
- Converted Leads: ~4K
- Conversion Rate: ~39%

### Top Performing Lead Sources
- Google generated the highest number of converted leads.
- Direct Traffic and Referral channels also contributed significantly to conversions.

### Funnel Analysis
- Approximately 39% of total leads were successfully converted.
- Around 61% of leads did not convert, indicating opportunities for optimization.

### Customer Segmentation
- Conversion performance varies across occupations and locations.
- Certain lead origins produce higher quality leads than others.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase investment in high-performing lead sources such as Google and Direct Traffic.

2. Optimize campaigns targeting low-converting channels to reduce acquisition costs.

3. Improve lead nurturing strategies for non-converted leads.

4. Focus marketing efforts on customer segments with higher conversion potential.

5. Continuously monitor funnel performance to identify and reduce drop-off points.

---

## Conclusion

This dashboard demonstrates how marketing funnel analysis can be used to improve lead conversion performance and support data-driven decision making. By identifying successful acquisition channels and understanding customer behavior, organizations can optimize marketing strategies and improve overall business growth.
